A Village Doctor

A village doctor works in medical stores in rural areas. He knows only how prescribe medicine to the villagers hearing their problems or diseases. He is not a well-trained doctor. Truly speaking he is a quack. In spite of it, he is the last hope to many villagers. The importance of a village doctor is set a room of a village market. There are possible measures to cure the diseases of the villagers. Generally, the dispensary of a village doctor is set in a room of a village market. There are medicines on the shelf of his dispensary. His dispensary is not rich. There are chairs, tables and other necessary things in his dispensary. The method of his treatment is very simple. He has a very small number of scientific apparatus like a stethoscope, a blood pressure machine, some injection instruments, etc. He does not know how to test blood or urine. He just sees the condition of a patient and gives him or her some medicines according to his knowledge. The daily life of a village doctor is very busy.
